# App Store Privacy Answers Draft

Last reviewed: July 16, 2026

Use this as a conservative draft when completing App Store Connect. Recheck the
questions shown by Apple at submission time and keep these answers aligned with
`VoiceJournal/Resources/PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy`.

## Tracking

Does Flara Day use data to track users across apps or websites owned by other
companies?

Recommended answer: **No**.

Flara Day has no advertising SDK, cross-app tracking, data broker integration,
or custom analytics SDK.

## Data Types To Disclose

### Audio Data

- Collected: Yes, when cloud transcription is used
- Linked to the user: No
- Used for tracking: No
- Purpose: App Functionality

Audio is transmitted to the Flara Day Worker and OpenAI to create live preview
or final transcription. Flara Day does not intentionally persist uploaded audio
on its backend after the request finishes.

### Other User Content

- Collected: Yes
- Linked to the user: Yes, disclose conservatively
- Used for tracking: No
- Purpose: App Functionality

Journal text may be transmitted for AI cleanup, title generation, and optional
translation into the language selected in Settings. The original is preserved
locally when a translation is requested. Future-
letter title/body content is associated with a verified email recipient when
email delivery is selected, so the category should be treated as linked even
though ordinary journal processing does not require an account.

### Email Address

- Collected: Yes, only for future-letter email delivery
- Linked to the user: Yes
- Used for tracking: No
- Purpose: App Functionality

The recipient address is verified before scheduling. The pending address is
encrypted with the scheduled letter. A one-way representation may be retained
for verification status, abuse prevention, and service reliability.

### Device ID

- Collected: Yes, an app-generated random device identifier
- Linked to the user: Yes, disclose conservatively
- Used for tracking: No
- Purpose: App Functionality

The identifier is generated by Flara Day, stored with a secret in the iOS
Keychain, and used to manage scheduled email letters without an account. It is
not Apple's advertising identifier and is not used across other companies' apps.

## Data Types Not Collected In This Build

- User ID: No account or sign-in exists.
- Purchases: No StoreKit purchase or subscription is active in beta.
- Product Interaction / Usage Data: No custom analytics is collected.
- Diagnostics: No third-party crash or diagnostics SDK is included.
- Precise Location, Contacts, Health, Fitness, Browsing, Search History: No.

Cloud providers may maintain security and reliability logs under their own
service terms. Flara Day's backend is designed not to log raw audio, complete
transcripts, journal bodies, verification codes, or device secrets.

## Local-Only Data

These items normally remain on the iPhone and are not reported as collected
unless the user chooses a feature that transmits them:

- saved journal entries and insight data
- appearance, lock, and memory-card settings
- feature-request board data
- in-app future letters and notification schedule

## Third-Party Processing Included In These Answers

- Cloudflare Workers and D1: API routing and encrypted pending email letters
- OpenAI: transcription, journal text processing, and optional translation
- Resend: verification codes and scheduled email delivery

## Current Release Assumptions

- no user accounts
- no subscriptions or paywall during selected-friends beta
- no ads, tracking, analytics, or third-party crash SDK
- support is provided by email, not an in-app support form
- future-letter email is available free during beta
