Flara Day Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Flara Day is a private voice journal app that helps you record, transcribe, and reflect on journal entries. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed, why it is processed, and the choices you have.

Information Flara Day Processes

Journal entries. Flara Day stores journal entries you create in the app, including text, titles, dates, mood indicators, and insight-related information. These entries are stored on your device unless you choose to export them.

Audio recordings. When you record a journal, the app temporarily processes the audio recording so it can be transcribed and turned into a draft journal entry. For cloud transcription and journal drafting, audio recordings may be sent to Flara Day's backend service and then to OpenAI for transcription and text processing.

Settings and preferences. Flara Day stores app preferences such as theme, font, font size, lock settings, memory card preference, and live preview preference on your device.

Security settings. If you enable Face ID or password lock, Flara Day uses those settings to help protect access to your journal on your device. Face ID authentication is handled by Apple; Flara Day does not receive or store your Face ID biometric data.

How Information Is Used

  • record and transcribe voice journals
  • create cleaned journal drafts
  • display journal history, calendar views, and insights
  • support live transcription preview while recording, if enabled
  • maintain app preferences and security settings
  • allow import, export, and deletion of journal data

Flara Day does not sell your journal content.

Cloud Processing

When cloud transcription or AI journal drafting is used, audio and text may be processed by Flara Day's backend service hosted on Cloudflare Workers and by OpenAI services for transcription and text processing.

Data Retention

Journal entries are stored on your device until you delete them or delete your account data in the app. Audio recordings are used to create transcription and journal drafts. Flara Day does not intentionally store uploaded audio recordings on its backend after processing. Cloud service providers may process request data according to their own service terms and retention practices.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete individual journal entries in the app. You can also delete all journals and local account/profile data from Settings.

Data Sharing

Flara Day shares information only as needed to provide app functionality, such as sending audio or text to cloud services for transcription and journal drafting. Flara Day does not use your journal content for advertising or tracking.

Tracking

Flara Day does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Children

Flara Day is not intended for children under 13.

Security

Flara Day uses reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including HTTPS for cloud backend communication and local device protections such as Face ID and password lock when enabled. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
