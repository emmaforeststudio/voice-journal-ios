# Flara Day Support

Last reviewed: July 20, 2026

For help with Flara Day, email `emmaforeststudio@gmail.com`.

## Recording Or Transcription Is Not Working

Cloud transcription needs an internet connection. Confirm microphone access in
iPhone Settings, stop and retry the recording, and check Settings > Connection
inside Flara Day. If Live Preview is off or unavailable, the final transcription
can still work after recording stops.

During the selected-friends beta, one recording can be up to 30 minutes and the
app allows up to 60 transcribed voice minutes per calendar day. Typed journals
and letters remain available after the daily voice allowance is reached.

An active recording can continue while the iPhone is locked or Flara Day is in
the background for the remainder of the same 30-minute recording limit. For
example, locking the iPhone after 8 minutes leaves up to 22 minutes. Screen lock
alone does not shorten the limit. A phone call, Siri, alarm, disconnected
microphone, force-quit, or iOS termination may stop the recording earlier;
Flara Day keeps and processes everything captured before a handled interruption.

Flara Day is designed to preserve common spoken languages, including mixed-
language speech. If words are omitted or changed to another language, include
the languages used and an approximate recording length in the support email. Do
not send private journal text unless you choose to.

## Where Journals Are Stored

Saved journals are stored on the iPhone unless exported. Audio and text are sent
to cloud services only for transcription and AI journal processing. In-app future
letters are also local. Email future letters are encrypted on the backend while
waiting for delivery.

## Future-Letter Notifications

Allow notifications in iPhone Settings > Notifications > Flara Day. For a test,
schedule a letter several minutes ahead. Tapping the notification should open
the delivered letter.

## Future-Letter Email

Select Email, enter the recipient address, request a verification code, and type
the code sent to that address. Only a verified address can receive a scheduled
letter. Check spam/junk if the code or letter does not arrive.

## Import And Export

Export Journals creates a shareable journal file. The importer accepts compatible
plain text, UTF-8 text, JSON, RTF, HTML, and PDFs containing selectable text.
Scanned image PDFs need OCR first. Word documents are not imported directly.
Apple Notes can be imported after the note is exported or shared as PDF or text.

## Editing Or Deleting Data

Delete an entry from its journal detail screen. Use Settings > Privacy to delete
journals or all app data. Pending future letters can be deleted from their list;
already delivered email cannot be recalled.

## App Lock

Face ID is handled by iOS and can be enabled in Settings. Flara Day also offers a
local password lock. Flara Day never receives Face ID biometric data.

## Feature Requests

Open Settings > Support > Request / Vote on Features. The current feature board
is local to this iPhone and is not a shared public voting service.
