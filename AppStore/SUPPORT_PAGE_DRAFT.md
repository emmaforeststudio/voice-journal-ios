## Common Questions

### My recording is not transcribing.

Check your internet connection and try again. Cloud transcription requires network access. If live preview is unavailable, your audio may still be recording and final transcription may still work.

### Where are my journals stored?

Journal entries are stored on your device unless you export them. Cloud processing is used for transcription and draft generation.

### How do I delete my journals?

Open Settings, then use the privacy section to delete journals or delete account data.

### How do I export my journals?

Open Settings, then choose Export Journals.

### How do I request a feature?

Open Settings, then Support, then Request / Vote on Features.
