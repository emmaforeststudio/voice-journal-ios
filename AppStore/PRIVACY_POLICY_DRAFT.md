# Flara Day Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Flara Day is a private voice journal app that helps you record, transcribe, and reflect on journal entries. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed, why it is processed, and the choices you have.

## Information Flara Day Processes

### Journal Entries

Flara Day stores journal entries you create in the app, including text, titles, dates, mood indicators, and insight-related information. These entries are stored on your device unless you choose to export them.

### Audio Recordings

When you record a journal, the app temporarily processes the audio recording so it can be transcribed and turned into a draft journal entry.

For cloud transcription and journal drafting, audio recordings may be sent to Flara Day's backend service and then to OpenAI for transcription and text processing. This is necessary to provide cloud transcription, live preview, and journal draft generation.

### Settings and Preferences

Flara Day stores app preferences such as theme, font, font size, lock settings, memory card preference, and live preview preference on your device.

### Security Settings

If you enable Face ID or password lock, Flara Day uses those settings to help protect access to your journal on your device. Face ID authentication is handled by Apple; Flara Day does not receive or store your Face ID biometric data.

### Imported and Exported Journals

If you import or export journals, Flara Day processes the files you choose for that action. Exported files are created only when you choose to export.

### Future Letters and Email Addresses

Future letters scheduled for in-app notification remain on your device. If you choose email delivery, Flara Day processes the recipient email address, a verification code, the letter title and body, and the requested delivery time. The email address must be verified before a letter can be scheduled.

## How Information Is Used

Flara Day uses information to:

- record and transcribe voice journals
- create cleaned journal drafts
- display journal history, calendar views, and insights
- support live transcription preview while recording, if enabled
- maintain app preferences and security settings
- allow import, export, and deletion of journal data
- verify an email address and deliver a future letter when you choose email delivery

Flara Day does not sell your journal content.

## Cloud Processing

When cloud transcription or AI journal drafting is used, audio and text may be processed by:

- Flara Day backend service hosted on Cloudflare Workers
- OpenAI services for transcription and text processing
- Resend for verification codes and future-letter email delivery

The Flara Day backend is designed to keep the OpenAI API key off your device and to forward requests needed for transcription and journal drafting.

Scheduled email letters are encrypted while waiting in Cloudflare D1. The app uses a random device identifier and secret stored in the iOS Keychain to manage scheduled letters without requiring a Flara Day account.

## Data Retention

Journal entries are stored on your device until you delete them or delete your account data in the app.

Audio recordings are used to create transcription and journal drafts. Flara Day does not intentionally store uploaded audio recordings on its backend after processing. Cloud service providers may process request data according to their own service terms and retention practices.

For future-letter email delivery, a one-way representation of the verified email address and delivery status may be retained to operate and secure the service. The full email address and letter content are encrypted while the letter is waiting to be delivered. After successful delivery or permanent delivery failure, the stored full letter content is replaced with status-only data. Canceling or deleting a letter removes its remote record. Verification requests expire and are retained only as needed for rate limiting, security, and reliability.

Feature requests and votes currently live locally on your device.

## Deleting Your Data

You can delete individual journal entries in the app. You can also delete all journals and local account/profile data from Settings.

Deleting a scheduled email letter before delivery asks the backend to cancel it and remove its stored full content. Deleting local app data removes journal data stored by Flara Day on your device. It does not delete email already delivered or data already processed by third-party service providers according to their separate policies.

## Data Sharing

Flara Day shares information only as needed to provide app functionality, such as sending audio or text for transcription and journal drafting, or sending a verified recipient address and future letter to Resend for email delivery.

Flara Day does not use your journal content for advertising or tracking.

## Tracking

Flara Day does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

## Children

Flara Day is not intended for children under 13.

## Security

Flara Day uses reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including HTTPS for cloud backend communication and local device protections such as Face ID and password lock when enabled. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted with a new effective date.
